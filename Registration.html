Rules and Information about BIS Poetry Slam

  • Individual poems should not exceed 3 minutes and group poems must not exceed 5 minutes.
  • Judges will be local poets, educators and literature lovers. Judges will not be affiliated with any of the participating schools. The main criteria that poets will be judges on are content, poetic conventions, memorization and overall delivery.
  • The winning team receives a beautiful bronze trophy. Individual winners win attractive prizes. Last yearâ€™s winners won Paperwhite Kindles!
  • Schools must complete registration protocol by the 15th of August, 2017.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Schools will nominate a team of 3 poets from grade 11 and 12 for the Slam.
  • Participating students must have an original poem to perform and also work on a group poem for the final round.
  • Schools must complete registration protocol by the 15th of August, 2017.
